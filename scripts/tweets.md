Use metadataBase + canonical URLs from day one. Fixing SEO after launch is painful. Getting it right upfront takes 2 minutes and saves weeks of indexing headaches.

## 6. I rebuilt my entire portfolio to be LLM-friendly.
Removed all framer-motion opacity:0 wrappers so AI crawlers (Google SGE, ChatGPT, Perplexity) can read the full page.
Result: content rendering went from 1% to 100%.
llms.txt + visible SSR = GEO done right.

## 7. Hot take: framer-motion is overused in portfolios.
That initial={{opacity:0}} wrapper on every section? It hides your content from AI crawlers.
If Google SGE can't read your site, you don't exist in AI search.
Use CSS transitions instead. Your content stays visible, your animations still work.
